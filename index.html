Who am I

Welcome to my webstie. My name is Isaiah, and I love everyting I.T. In fact, the website that you are looking at was coded by me. I did the whole thing by hand with no A.I help in any way. However, that does not put me out of date. I am currenly studing on how to be an ethical hacker. That means that I am always doing my best to stay up to date on the latest developments and trends in the I.T world. It also means that security is a high priority for me. I often make it a habit to do things the best practice way, and if I do not know what that way is, I will go and find out.

If you are confused and do not know where to begin browsing this website, then I reccomend that you start in articles. Articles are going to make up the bulk of this website. They can contain anything from a discussion on a I.T topic, to making statements about conversial technologies, to talking over a moment that I learned from. Projects, on the other hand, are step-by-step instructions on how to build something the way I did. I also take the time to explain why I did it that way.

Before you go off clicking every link in this website, there is something that you should keep in mind. This website is still under heavy construction. So if things do not work the way they are suposed to, I apologize for the inconvience.
